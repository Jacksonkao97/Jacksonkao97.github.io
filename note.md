## 3 Guidelines for Writing FAQ Questions
> 1. Use 'I' and 'my' when writing the questions:
    - How do I fork in GitHub?
    - Does my MacBook need XCode installed?
> 2. Start with a question word.
    - Can I use Markdown with YAML?
    - What is the relationship between Markdown and YAML?
> 3. Write for your audience, not other computer scientists.
    - Can I pay my tuition online?
    - Can I use my user portal as a payment gateway?
> ### Questions words:
> - What
> - How
> - Which
> - When
> - Where
> - Whom
> - Can
> - Why
## 4 Guidelines for Writing FAQ Answers
> 1. Answer the question!
> 2. For questions, requiring a Yes or No answer:
    - Answer with 'Yes' or 'No' and then provide an elaboration.
> 3. For questions that require instructions in the answer:
    - Give a simple answer, if it is available.
    - If the answer is not simple, provide a link to the appropriate documentation.
> 4. Repeat part of the question in the answer.
